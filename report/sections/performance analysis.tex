\subsection{Training pipeline}
The training pipeline is implemented in train.py, which includes training hyperparameter components, model settings, checkpoint saving, and training monitoring and history recording through wandb.


The hyperparameter setting obtained from Task1 is partially reused in the segmentation task as a reference. Specifically, the AdamW optimizer is retained due to their effectiveness in transfer learning.

However, due to the higher computational cost and memory consumption of pixel-wise segmentation, the batch size is reduced, and the number of training epochs is adjusted accordingly to ensure stable convergence.

Therefore, we consider a batchsize of 16 and an early stopping strategy for a total 50 epoches.


So, the final configuration is as follow:

\[
\text{optimizer} = \text{AdamW}, \quad
\text{lr} = 5*10^{-4}, \quad
\]
\[
\text{weight decay} = 10^{-4}, \quad
\text{batch size} = 16, \quad
\text{epochs} = 50.
\]
