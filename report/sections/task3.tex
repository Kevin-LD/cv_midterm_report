\section{Task 3: Pixel-level Training Of Image Segmentation Models}

In this task, we build a classic semantic segmentation network U-net, which includes the complete downsampling encoder, upsampling decoder and SkipConnection on Lightweight three-class segmentation dataset Oxford-IIIT Pet Dataset. With fixed training parameters and random initialization of weights, we aim at comparing the performance of models with 3 different loss functions. 

To make comparison, we compare the mIoU performance on the validation set, the loss curve on training and validation set and also the accuracy on validation and testing set.
